Features

Everything you need in a cloud-native banking platform and more.

100% Cloud-Native

Built entirely on Kubernetes with containerized microservices, auto-scaling capabilities, and seamless deployment across any cloud provider or on-premises infrastructure.

GitOps-Driven CI/CD

Complete GitOps workflow with automated testing, building, and deployment pipelines. Track every change with full observability and instant rollbacks when needed.

Complete Observability

Comprehensive monitoring with Prometheus metrics, beautiful Grafana dashboards, and centralized logging with Loki. Full visibility into your banking platform's health.

Operations

DevOps excellence made simple, yet powerful.

Deploy anywhere, instantly! GitOps-powered, zero-downtime deployments.

Push to Git and watch your changes automatically flow through our GitOps pipeline. Kubernetes handles the orchestration while Helm manages your releases. Blue-green deployments ensure zero downtime for your users.

Scale up or down automatically based on real-time demand.

Horizontal Pod Autoscaler (HPA) and Vertical Pod Autoscaler (VPA) work together to ensure optimal resource utilization. Your banking platform scales seamlessly during peak hours and saves costs during low traffic.

Complete visibility with Prometheus, Grafana, and Loki integration.

Real-time metrics, beautiful dashboards, and intelligent alerting keep you informed. Distributed tracing helps debug issues across microservices while centralized logging with Loki provides comprehensive audit trails.
